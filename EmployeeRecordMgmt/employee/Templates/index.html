Audit Management System


The system is built on the Django framework, leveraging its capabilities to provide a user-friendly interface and complex database structure for managing relational data. Display a historical record of all modifications made to employee or student data. Log all activities within the production environment for security and compliance purposes. Enable/disable auditing for specific features through a configuration-driven system. Administrators can choose which actions to audit, such as inquiries or updates.
